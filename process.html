Our Process

Here’s what you can expect after making a booking with us — a simple, transparent 8-step process.

1. Initial Inquiry

We receive your booking request and review your project needs to understand your goals.

2. Discovery Call

We schedule a short 15–20 minute meeting to discuss your goals, requirements, and vision.

3. Quote & Proposal

You receive a clear project quote outlining pricing, timeline, and what’s included.

4. Confirmation & Deposit

Once confirmed, a deposit is paid and your project is officially scheduled.

5. Planning & Preparation

We collect all required information, content, or inspiration before the project begins.

6. Project Begins

We start working on your project and provide updates throughout key stages.

7. Review & Revisions

You review the results and request any changes required to get everything perfect.

8. Completion & Delivery

Your project is completed, delivered, and supported to ensure everything meets expectations.
